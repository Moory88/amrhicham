Profile

A retail operations and client experience leader with 15 years at the premium end — across Apple, Marks & Spencer under the Al-Futtaim Group, and luxury brand consulting. The thread across all of it: measurable commercial outcomes, teams that outperform, and clients who return. At Apple Yas Mall, Amr progressed from Technical Specialist through to Lead Genius, earning the highest Employee Engagement Score in EMEA and the store's highest-ever NPS — before being hand-selected as Global NSO Support Mentor for Apple's landmark India flagship entry at BKC Mumbai. As Principal Consultant at Arete Retail Consultancy, he designed UHNWI clienteling programmes for ultra-premium brands including Azza Fahmy and Kojak Studio, and led talent assessment for the Emax Apple shop-in-shop UAE launch. Prior to that, seven years with M&S across Cairo and Abu Dhabi produced 12% year-on-year sales growth for three consecutive years and the recognition for Best Visual Merchandising Store in the UAE. Today, those same instincts — active listening, precise needs profiling, composure under pressure, and a relentless attention to the client — are directed toward Dubai's property market at Huspy. Bilingual in English and Arabic. Native to both.
